# GitLog AI - Go-to-Market Strategy

## Target Audience

1. **Primary**: Open Source Maintainers (OSS)
2. **Secondary**: DevOps Engineers, Product Managers
3. **Tertiary**: Solo Developers, Indie Hackers

## Value Proposition

> "Stop writing changelogs by hand. Let AI do the boring work."

- **Pain Point**: Writing release notes is tedious and error-prone.
- **Solution**: GitLog AI analyzes commits and generates polished changelogs instantly.

## Competitive Analysis

| Competitor      | Pricing       | Key Difference                     |
|-----------------|---------------|------------------------------------|
| release-it      | Free (CLI)    | CLI-only, no AI                    |
| semantic-release| Free (CLI)    | Complex setup, automated releases  |
| Changelogfy     | Paid SaaS     | Manual changelog management        |
| **GitLog AI**   | Free (BYOK)   | AI-powered, zero friction, BYOK    |
